About Us

Nanjing Hoongyi Information Technology Co., Ltd. is a technology company centered on the e-commerce industry, committed to promoting the digitalization and intelligence of the entire e-commerce industry chain. Leveraging advanced technology and extensive industry experience, the company provides comprehensive e-commerce solutions, including software platforms, technical services, and consulting and commercial services. Through means such as investment and incubation, the company aims to facilitate the implementation, aggregation, and development of the e-commerce industry, helping enterprises reduce costs and increase efficiency, and accelerating project implementation.

The company's main services target e-commerce enterprises, brand merchants, factory manufacturers, service providers, and e-commerce platforms. Key industries include cross-border e-commerce, rural e-commerce, consumer goods e-commerce, and MCN institutions. We offers a range of services from e-commerce ERP systems to advanced AI modules, supporting integrated management of key links such as multi-platform store docking, supply chain management, and order management. Additionally, the company provides consulting and commercial services including legal and regulatory policy consultation, product selection consultation, supply chain optimization, and multi-channel sales strategy consultation, as well as investment incubation services such as angel investment, office space support, and shared warehousing and logistics bases, comprehensively aiding the rapid development of e-commerce enterprises.
